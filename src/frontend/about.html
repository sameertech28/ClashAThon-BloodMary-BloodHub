Our Mission

We Built BLOODHUB to Save Lives

Eliminating preventable deaths due to blood unavailability in Nepal — one matched donation at a time.

Mission

To Eliminate Preventable Deaths

Every year, thousands of Nepalis die because the right blood wasn't at the right place at the right time. Not because there were no donors — but because there was no smart system to connect them.

BLOODHUB replaces the chaotic 50-phone-call emergency response with a real-time, algorithmic matching system that connects hospitals with compatible, nearby donors in seconds.

Vision

A Nepal Where No One Dies Without Blood

We envision a Nepal where every hospital has instant access to a network of verified, ready donors — and where no patient dies waiting for a phone to ring.

Connecting every patient with a donor within 5 minutes
50 hospitals and 5,000 donors in Year 1
Reducing response time from 3–4 hours to under 5 minutes
