The Process

How BLOODHUB Matching Works

A seamless 5-step automated pipeline from emergency need to donor response.

1

Hospital Creates Request

Verified hospital logs in and submits: blood type, quantity, urgency, city.

2

Smart Matching

System filters donors by exact blood type AND same city simultaneously.

3

Instant Alerts

Matched donors see requests on their dashboard. An automated alert is sent to their email notifying them of the specific hospital and city in need.

4

One-Click Response

Donor clicks "I'M COMING" — their contact info and location availability are instantly shared with the hospital.

5

Hospital Acts

Hospital accesses a real-time roster of all responders to coordinate the donation and save the patient's life.

Features

Every Feature Built for Emergencies

Smart Matching Engine

Dual-filter algorithm matches donors by blood type AND location simultaneously — zero irrelevant alerts.

Instant Email & Dashboard Alerts

Donors receive automated email notifications and see matching requests the moment they are published. No delays, no phone tag.

Real-Time Hospital View

Hospitals get a live responders table updated as donors click "I'M COMING". No refresh needed.

Verified Donors

Every donor completes a health eligibility declaration ensuring blood safety standards are met.

Hospital Authentication

Strict hospital verification process — only government-licensed health facilities can create requests.

One-Click Donor Response

Donors respond to emergencies with a single click. Their contact details go straight to the hospital.
