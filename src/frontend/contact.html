FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly do you respond to partnership inquiries?

Hospital partnership applications are typically reviewed and responded to within 1–2 business days. For the demo, all hospitals are auto-verified upon registration.

Is there a cost to join as a hospital or donor?

BLOODHUB is completely free for donors. Hospitals during the initial launch period also have free access. A sustainability model with premium features is planned for Year 2.

How do I report a technical issue?

Use the contact form above and select "Technical Support / Bug" as the topic. Please include your browser, device and a description of the issue. For urgent platform issues email us directly at tech@bloodhub.org.np.
